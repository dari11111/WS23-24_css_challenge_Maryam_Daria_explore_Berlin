The most beautiful places in Berlin

Do you want to know some amazing place to visit in Berlin? Then read this article and find out where to spend next weekends in Berlin!

Nature

If you like nature, we highly recommend you to go to:

  1. Grünewald
  2. Britzer Garten
  3. Tiergarten
  4. Treptower Park
Place Description Image Location
Grünewald A vast forest area with beautiful hiking trails and stunning views. View on Google Maps
Britzer Garten A large park with colorful flower displays, walking paths, and a lake. View on Google Maps
Tiergarten A central park in Berlin known for its green spaces and iconic monuments. View on Google Maps
Treptower Park A riverside park with walking paths, a Soviet War Memorial, and gardens. View on Google Maps
